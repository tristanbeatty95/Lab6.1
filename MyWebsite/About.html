A Little More About Me

This is an autobiography from me! Yes this is indeed an autobiography just all about how wonderful being a Triscuit is. As a low sodium, whole wheat cracker, I often wonder what life would be like if I was a wheat thin. People tend to like them more, but I have my own little dedicated fan base where people pretend to like me because I'm the "healthier" option. Grass is always greener I suppose....

Sometimes I come in different flavors, and I love that for me! Because just plain old triscuits can be a little bland. I am bland. I honestly don't really know what I am typing at this point or why I am typing it. So I will end this sillyness really soon, I just need to make sure there is enough content here to show that I am an HTML Wizard. Cya.
